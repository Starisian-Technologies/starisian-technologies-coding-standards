# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors must keep interactions respectful, professional, and inclusive.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and specific in technical feedback.
- Focus on standards quality and enforcement evidence.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict access for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to repository maintainers privately through the security contact channel in `SECURITY.md`.
